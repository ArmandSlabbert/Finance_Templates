## Overview
This directory contains the official Standard Operating Procedure governing how financial and operational records are retained, secured, and made available for audit.

The objective is to ensure documentation is:

- accessible  
- complete  
- secure  
- legally compliant  
- audit ready  

Strong evidence management reduces audit time, cost, and disruption.

---

## What This SOP Covers
The procedure establishes mandatory controls for:

- Retention periods  
- Audit evidence standards  
- Storage requirements  
- Access governance  
- Audit file preparation  
- Disposal of expired records  

It applies to **all financial, tax, payroll, operational, and governance documents**, whether electronic or physical.

## Core Activities in This SOP

### Retention Governance
- maintain statutory minimum retention  
- review schedules periodically  
- retain contracts through validity periods  

### Audit Evidence
- ensure completeness and accuracy  
- support transactions and disclosures  
- cross-reference to GL  

### Storage & Protection
- secure physical and digital storage  
- perform regular backups  
- control user access  

### Audit Support
- maintain structured annual audit files  
- log requests and responses  
- deliver information on time  

### Disposal
- securely destroy expired records  
- ensure compliance with data protection  
- document approvals
