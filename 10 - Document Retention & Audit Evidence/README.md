# SOP 10 – Document Retention & Audit Evidence

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing the storage, retention, and accessibility of
financial documentation.

The objective is to ensure evidence supporting financial reporting,
tax submissions, and control activities is complete, secure, and
available for audit and regulatory review.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_10_Document_Retention_and_Audit_Evidence.docx**  
Defines retention responsibilities, documentation standards, and
access protocols.

### Operational Checklist
• **SOP_10_Document_Retention_and_Audit_Evidence_Checklist.xlsx**  
A structured tracker used to confirm documents are filed, complete,
and audit ready.

## Key Areas Covered
• Month-end workpapers  
• Journal support  
• Bank reconciliations  
• VAT and tax returns  
• Payroll documentation  
• Contracts and agreements  

## How This Is Used
The SOP defines governance expectations, while the checklist is used
to monitor completeness and readiness for internal and external audits.

## Outputs
• Organised documentation  
• Clear ownership  
• Rapid audit retrieval  

## Standards
ISA – Audit Evidence  
Companies Act & SARS requirements
