# SOP 07 – Prepayments & Accruals

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing the recognition, calculation, posting,
reversal, and review of accruals and prepayments.

The objective is to ensure expenses are recorded in the correct
reporting period in accordance with the accrual basis of accounting.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_07_Prepayments_and_Accruals.docx**  
Defines responsibilities, calculation principles, posting rules, and
review requirements.

### Operational Checklist
• **SOP_07_Prepayments_and_Accruals_Checklist.xlsx**  
A structured control checklist used to prepare, validate, and track
accruals and prepayments.

## Key Areas Covered
• Identification of accruals  
• Estimation methodology  
• Prepayment amortisation  
• Supporting documentation  
• Reversal procedures  
• GL accuracy  

## How This Is Used
The SOP defines the policy, while the checklist is completed each
reporting period to ensure consistency, transparency, and audit
readiness.

Completed files are retained as month-end close evidence.

## Outputs
• Accurate expense recognition  
• Reconciled accrual and prepayment balances  
• Clear movement schedules  

## Standards
IFRS – Accrual Basis of Accounting  
IAS 1 – Presentation of Financial Statements
