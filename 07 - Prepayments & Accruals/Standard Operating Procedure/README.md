# SOP 07 – Pre-payments & Accruals

## Overview
Standard Operating Procedure governing the identification,
recognition, and review of pre-payments and accruals to ensure
expenses are recorded in the correct accounting period.

## Key Areas
• Pre-payment identification and amortisation
• Accrual estimation and recognition
• Month-end review and reconciliations
• Management review and sign-off

## Standards
IAS 1 – Presentation of Financial Statements
