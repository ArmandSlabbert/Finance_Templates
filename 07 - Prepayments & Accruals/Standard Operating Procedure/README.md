## Overview
This directory contains the official Standard Operating Procedure governing the identification, calculation, posting, and review of pre-payments and accruals.

The objective is to ensure expenses are:

- recognised in the correct accounting period  
- accurate and complete  
- supported by reasonable evidence  
- compliant with the accrual basis of accounting  

These adjustments are fundamental to reliable month-end reporting.

---

## What This SOP Covers
The procedure establishes mandatory controls for:

- Identifying timing differences  
- Recognition principles  
- Amortisation of pre-payments  
- Estimation of accruals  
- Journal preparation  
- Monthly reconciliations  
- Review and approval  
- Documentation standards  

It applies to **all operating expenses and supplier transactions**.

## Control Principles Embedded in This SOP

### 1) Period Accuracy
Expenses must be recorded when incurred, not when paid.

### 2) Supportable Estimates
Accruals require logical and defendable calculations.

### 3) Systematic Release
Pre-payments must amortise over benefit periods.

### 4) Transparency
Schedules must clearly show movements.

### 5) Independent Oversight
Finance Manager review is mandatory.

---

## Core Activities in This SOP

### Pre-payments
- Identify advance payments  
- record as assets  
- amortise over contract or usage period  
- reassess monthly  

### Accruals
- identify uninvoiced expenses  
- estimate using contracts or trends  
- record in correct period  
- reverse or adjust upon invoice receipt  

### Month-End Controls
- reconcile balances  
- compare to prior periods and budgets  
- investigate unusual or material changes  
- obtain Finance Manager sign-off
