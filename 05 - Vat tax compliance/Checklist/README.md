# SOP 05 – VAT & Tax Compliance

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing VAT and statutory tax compliance.

The objective is to ensure accurate calculation, timely submission,
proper approval, and full supporting documentation for all tax
obligations.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_05_VAT_and_Tax_Compliance.docx**  
Defines the policies, responsibilities, and controls for tax
reconciliations, return preparation, submission, and payment.

### Operational Checklist
• **VAT_and_Tax_Compliance_Checklist_SOP_05.xlsx**  
A structured checklist and submission tracker used to evidence tax
calculations, approvals, filings, and payments.

## Key Areas Covered
• Output and input VAT reconciliation  
• VAT201 preparation and submission  
• PAYE, UIF, and SDL reconciliation  
• Provisional and income tax calculations  
• Payment approvals and confirmations  
• SARS compliance and audit trail  

## How This Is Used
The SOP defines the governance framework, while the checklist is used
operationally to track preparation, review, submission, and payment of
tax obligations.

Completed checklists are retained as part of month-end and statutory
compliance records.

## Outputs
• Submitted tax returns  
• Confirmed payments or refund positions  
• Reconciled tax control accounts  

## Standards
VAT Act (South Africa)  
Income Tax Act (South Africa)
